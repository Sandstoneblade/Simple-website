About Deeth's Coffee Place

Founded in 2015 in Normal Illinois, our coffee shop prides itself in being a fair-trade company, supporting farmers with a fair price being paid for their produce.

We take pride in our service, and our modest beginnings. We hope you visit our coffee place and enjoy a cup.
